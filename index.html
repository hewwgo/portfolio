UIST 2026 in review

Tessera: Faceted Exploration of Collective Knowledge for Collaborative Ideation

Hugo Andersson, Niklas Elmqvist

collective knowledge group ideation faceted exploration LLM

Abstract

Tessera captures a research group's collective knowledge as a navigable map of tiles, then launches a faceted ideation engine that generates candidate directions constrained by the group's actual submissions. The LLM drives divergence by inferring dimensions; the user drives convergence by locking in constraints, with the exploration space restructuring at each step. Deployed with a university research group over two weeks.

AVI 2026 · Venice accepted

TombWriter: Scaffolding Story Archeology through Beat-Level Interaction in Human-AI Co-Writing

Hugo Andersson, Niklas Elmqvist

creative writing beat-level interaction narrative creativity support

Abstract

TombWriter treats LLM prompts not as disposable queries but as a hierarchical story instrument refined over time. Writers work at the level of story beats rather than prose — generating character actions and editing to excavate their intended story. A qualitative study with five experienced writers over three days found writers framed the AI as a generation engine rather than a collaborator, and valued the system for structural discovery.
