hugo andersson

I'm a PhD Fellow in Human-Centered Computing at Aarhus University, advised by Niklas Elmqvist. I build systems where human and artificial reasoning mix, then study what happens to the thinking. My work combines HCI, AI, and design research, exploring what happens to knowledge, creativity, and meaning when people and AI share the same cognitive environment. I'm particularly drawn to agentic AI and the near future these systems point toward, not just the interfaces we put on them.
